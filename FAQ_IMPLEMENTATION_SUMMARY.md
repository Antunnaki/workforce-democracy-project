**Answer**: Likely decrease with housing cooperatives. Berlin co-ops: 30-40% lower rent than private. Vienna: 60% cooperative housing keeps entire market affordable. Not forced, just competitive alternatives.

#### 3. **"Do I lose my rental property?"**
**Answer**: No. Worker democracy focuses on workplace ownership, not seizing property. Voluntary evolution through economic choice. Examples: Basque Country, Northern Italy have thriving private + cooperative coexistence.

**Answer**: Gradual (20-50 years), voluntary, incentive-based. Tax benefits, financing, market competition. Italian Marcora Law: 257 buyouts, 9,300 jobs saved, 94% success rate. No revolution, just evolution.
