**Input Font Sizes**:
```css

  font-size: 1rem;  /* 16px - prevents iOS zoom */
```

**Why 16px Matters**:
- iOS Safari auto-zooms on inputs < 16px
- 16px minimum prevents disruptive zoom behavior
- Better user experience on mobile

**Typography Scale**:
- h1: `var(--font-size-3xl)` (scales responsively)
- h2: `var(--font-size-2xl)`
- h3: `var(--font-size-xl)`
- Body: `var(--font-size-base)` (16px)
- Small: `var(--font-size-sm)`

**Assessment**: ‚úÖ **PERFECT** - Font sizes prevent iOS zoom and remain readable
