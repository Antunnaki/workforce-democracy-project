Civic Features Explained

  1. Country & Level Selection - Start by choosing your country (US, UK, Australia, etc.) and government level (federal, state/provincial, or local)

  5. Representative Search - Find officials by name, district, or use the search tool to discover representatives
  6. Court Decisions - Browse recent Supreme Court rulings with summaries and legal context

Understanding Workplace Democracy

Democratic workplaces typically feature:

  • Worker ownership - Employees own shares or stakes in the company
  • Participatory decision-making - Workers have a voice in major company decisions
  • Profit sharing - Surplus/profits distributed among all workers
  • Elected leadership - Managers and executives may be elected by workers
  • Transparent operations - Open books policy and shared information
