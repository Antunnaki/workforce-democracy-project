Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real app?

Yes! It's called a Progressive Web App (PWA). It works exactly like a native app but doesn't require the App Store or Google Play. PWAs update automatically, use less storage, and respect your privacy.

Does it cost anything?

No! It's completely free, just like our website. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. 100% free forever.

How much storage does it use?

Very little! Usually less than 5MB. Traditional native apps can be 50-200MB or more. PWAs are much more efficient.

Will it work offline?

Yes! Pages you've already visited are cached, so you can access them without internet. Loading new content requires a connection.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! We don't track you, sell data, or use analytics. Everything stays on your device. Check our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I uninstall it?

iOS: Long-press the icon ‚Üí Remove App
Android: Long-press the icon ‚Üí Uninstall
Desktop: Right-click icon ‚Üí Uninstall, or use Settings ‚Üí Apps

Will I get updates?

Yes! Updates happen automatically in the background. You don't need to do anything. No app store approvals, no waiting‚Äîjust instant updates when we improve the site.
