üéØ Our Mission

The Workforce Democracy Project provides a curated news feed from independent, factually accurate, and ethically transparent news organizations. This document explains exactly how we select and vet our sources.

‚úÖ Inclusion Criteria

To be included in our news feed, a source must meet ALL of these requirements:

1. Factual Accuracy ‚≠ê (MANDATORY)

  • Minimum Rating: "High Factual Accuracy" or above
  • Verification Source: Media Bias/Fact Check
  • Why: Factual accuracy is non-negotiable for informed civic participation

2. Editorial Transparency üìã (MANDATORY)

  • Funding Disclosure: Clearly states funding sources on website
  • Ownership Transparency: Discloses who owns/controls the organization
  • Editorial Independence: States editorial policies publicly
  • Why: Readers deserve to know who funds the news they read

3. Corrections Policy ‚úèÔ∏è (MANDATORY)

  • Publishes Corrections: Has public corrections/retractions policy
  • Acknowledges Errors: Corrects mistakes promptly and visibly
  • Why: All journalists make mistakes; ethical ones own up to them

4. Accessibility üåê (MANDATORY)

  • No Hard Paywall: Free tier available OR no paywall
  • Ethical Paywalls OK: Reader-supported models (donations/subscriptions) are fine if basic news is free
  • Why: Information should be accessible to all, regardless of income

5. Source Attribution üîó (MANDATORY)

  • Cites Sources: Clearly attributes information to sources
  • Links to Evidence: Provides links to original documents/data when possible
  • Why: Verifiable journalism requires transparent sourcing

6. Independence üóΩ (MANDATORY)

  • Not State-Controlled: Not owned/controlled by government
  • Exception: Public-funded broadcasters OK if editorially independent (BBC, NPR, ABC)
  • Why: Independent journalism holds power accountable

üìä Bias Transparency

We do not exclude sources based on political bias, as long as they meet factual accuracy standards.

Our Approach:

  • ‚úÖ Label bias clearly - Every source shows its bias rating
  • ‚úÖ Prioritize accuracy - Factual accuracy matters more than political leaning
  • ‚úÖ Include diverse perspectives - Left, center, and right sources (all factually accurate)
  • ‚úÖ Let users choose - Filter by category, not by our preferences

Bias Rating Scale (Media Bias/Fact Check):

Left Progressive/liberal perspective
Left-Center Slightly left-leaning
Least Biased Minimal editorial bias
Right-Center Slightly right-leaning
Right Conservative perspective

All sources must have "High" or "Very High" factual accuracy regardless of bias.
