üîê How We Protect Your Privacy

Fortress-Level Encryption (V32.7)

All data stored on your device is encrypted using AES-256-GCM, the same encryption standard used by governments and military organizations worldwide. Your encryption key never leaves your device.

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (Advanced Encryption Standard with Galois/Counter Mode)
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2-SHA-256 with 600,000 iterations (OWASP 2025 standard)
  • Random Salt: 16-byte cryptographically secure random salt per encryption
  • Initialization Vector: 12-byte random IV for each operation (prevents pattern detection)
  • Implementation: Web Crypto API (native browser security, hardware-accelerated)
  • Key Storage: Device-only, never transmitted or stored on servers
  • Data Categories: Political votes, chat history, searches, and preferences all encrypted separately

Why This Matters: Politicians, campaigns, and data brokers would pay significant amounts for your political preferences. Our encryption ensures that even if someone gained physical access to your device, your political data would be completely unreadable without your encryption key.
