## Design Philosophy

### Core Concepts Represented

**1. Democracy in Action:**
- **Central Table/Circle:** Represents democratic gathering space where decisions are made together
- **Star at Center:** Five points representing participation, voice, representation, transparency, and empowerment
- **Circular Layout:** Everyone has equal standing - no hierarchy

**2. Diverse Participation:**
- **8 Detailed People:** Representing diverse workforce and stakeholders
- **Color Variety:** Blue (civic), Green (growth), Purple (empowerment), Orange (warmth)
- **Network Connections:** Subtle lines showing interconnection and collaboration

**3. Warmth & Welcome:**
- **Warm Color Palette:** Orange, amber, and warm yellows instead of cool blues
- **Soft Glows:** Subtle lighting effects for approachability
- **Rounded Forms:** Friendly, non-threatening shapes

**4. Detail & Precision:**
- **Detailed Figures:** Each person has head, body, arms, and legs (vs simple stick figures)
- **White Outlines:** Crisp definition on each element
- **Shadows:** Depth and dimension for professional appearance
- **Multiple Gradients:** Rich, layered colors instead of flat tones

## Technical Improvements

### 1. Enhanced Detail

**Old:**
```svg
<!-- Simple person -->
<circle cx="0" cy="0" r="4" fill="#4A90E2"/>
<path d="M -3 4 L -3 12 M 0 6 L 0 12 M 3 4 L 3 12" 
      stroke="#4A90E2" stroke-width="1.5"/>
```

**New:**
```svg
<!-- Detailed person -->
<circle cx="0" cy="0" r="5" fill="url(#blueGradient)"/>
<circle cx="0" cy="0" r="5" fill="none" stroke="#fff" stroke-width="1"/>
<rect x="-2" y="5" width="4" height="10" rx="2" 
      fill="url(#blueGradient)" opacity="0.9"/>
<path d="M -4 7 L -6 10 M 4 7 L 6 10" 
      stroke="url(#blueGradient)" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round"/>
<path d="M -1 15 L -3 20 M 1 15 L 3 20" 
      stroke="url(#blueGradient)" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round"/>
```

**Improvements:**
- Larger head (5px vs 4px radius)
- White outline for definition
- Rounded rectangle for body (not just a line)
- Separate arms (not combined with body)
- Separate legs (more realistic proportions)
- Gradient fills instead of flat colors

### 3. Added Visual Effects

**Soft Glow (New):**
```svg
<filter id="softGlow">
  <feGaussianBlur stdDeviation="1.5" result="coloredBlur"/>
  <feMerge>
    <feMergeNode in="coloredBlur"/>
    <feMergeNode in="SourceGraphic"/>
  </feMerge>
</filter>
```

**Drop Shadow (New):**
```svg
<filter id="dropShadow">
  <feGaussianBlur in="SourceAlpha" stdDeviation="2"/>
  <feOffset dx="0" dy="2" result="offsetblur"/>
  <feComponentTransfer>
    <feFuncA type="linear" slope="0.3"/>
  </feComponentTransfer>
  <feMerge>
    <feMergeNode/>
    <feMergeNode in="SourceGraphic"/>
  </feMerge>
</filter>
```

**Benefits:**
- **Soft Glow:** Welcoming, approachable feel
- **Drop Shadow:** Depth, dimension, professionalism
- **Subtle Effects:** Not overwhelming, just enhancing

## Symbolism Breakdown

### The 8 People

**Position & Color Coding:**

1. **Top (Blue):** Civic Engagement - Democratic participation
2. **Top-Right (Green):** Growth - Economic prosperity  
3. **Right (Purple):** Empowerment - Worker dignity
4. **Bottom-Right (Orange):** Warmth - Welcoming community
5. **Bottom (Blue):** Unity - Collective strength
6. **Bottom-Left (Green):** Cooperation - Shared success
7. **Left (Purple):** Participation - Active involvement
8. **Top-Left (Orange):** Welcome - Inclusive culture

**Design Choice:** Mix of colors shows diversity and equal representation - no single identity dominates.

---

### The Central Star

**5 Points Represent:**

1. **Participation:** Everyone has a voice
2. **Voice:** All voices are heard
3. **Representation:** Fair and equal representation
4. **Transparency:** Open and honest processes
5. **Empowerment:** Power distributed to workers

**Why a Star?**
- Universal symbol of guidance and aspiration
- Five points match five core democratic principles
- Radiates outward - sharing light/knowledge
- Center of attention - focal point of democracy

---

### The Circular Table

**Why a Circle?**
- **No hierarchy:** No head of table
- **Equal access:** Everyone can see everyone
- **Democratic tradition:** Round table discussions
- **Inclusive:** No one is outside the circle

**Design Details:**
- Warm orange gradient (welcoming)
- Transparent fill (not blocking, open)
- Thick stroke (substantial, important)
- Size allows all 8 people to "sit" around it
