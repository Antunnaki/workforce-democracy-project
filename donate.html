Why Cryptocurrency Only?

We're committed to your privacy. Traditional payment processors (credit cards, PayPal, etc.) require extensive tracking and data sharing that conflicts with our core values of privacy and transparency.

Cryptocurrency donations are secure, private, and aligned with our mission. We've selected the most ethical options available:

  • Nano - Zero fees, instant, carbon-neutral (most ethical)

How to Make a One-Time Donation

  1. 1
    Get a Crypto Wallet
    Download a wallet app like Coinbase, Kraken, or Trust Wallet. Takes about 10 minutes to set up.
  2. 2
    Buy Cryptocurrency
    You can buy crypto with a debit card directly in most wallet apps. Start with as little as $5!
  3. 3
    Send to Our Address
    Copy one of our wallet addresses above and paste it into your wallet's "Send" function. Enter the amount you'd like to donate and confirm!

How to Set Up Recurring Donations

  1. 1
    Use a Wallet with Scheduling
    Some wallets like Coinbase allow you to schedule recurring sends. Set up a monthly or weekly transfer to our address.
  2. 2
    Set a Calendar Reminder
    If your wallet doesn't support scheduling, set a monthly reminder on your phone to send a donation manually. Takes just 30 seconds!
  3. 3
    Every Amount Helps
    Even $1, $5, or $10 per month adds up and makes a huge difference in sustaining this work and supporting our community programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will any features be locked behind donations?

Absolutely not. This site will remain 100% free forever. Every tool, every resource, every feature is and always will be accessible to everyone, regardless of whether they can donate. Donations are purely voluntary and only help us sustain and expand what we offer for free.

How will donations be used?

Donations support the ongoing development and maintenance of this platform, allow more time to be dedicated to building new features and resources, and fund community programs supporting democratic workplaces. Any excess beyond what's needed for sustainable development is reinvested into expanding our reach and supporting community organizations.

Is my donation anonymous?

Yes! Cryptocurrency donations are pseudonymous by default. We cannot see your identity, only the wallet address that sent the donation. If you want to remain completely anonymous, use a privacy-focused wallet. We will never ask for your personal information.

Why don't you use Patreon or similar platforms?

Platforms like Patreon, PayPal, and Stripe all require extensive user tracking and data sharing. They also take 5-12% fees from donations. We chose cryptocurrency to maintain our commitment to privacy, keep 100% of donations, and align with our values of transparency and decentralization.

I don't have crypto and can't donate. Can I still help?

Absolutely! The best way to support this project is to use it, share it with others who might find it valuable, and contribute to our community discussions. Your engagement and feedback are invaluable. When card payment options become available (via Proton's system), we'll let you know!

Can I see how much has been donated?

We're considering adding a transparency dashboard in the future that shows donation totals and how funds are being used. This aligns with our values of transparency. For now, we're focused on building out the core features. Stay tuned!

What if I change my mind about recurring donations?

You have complete control! Simply stop the scheduled sends in your wallet or remove the calendar reminder. There's no subscription to cancel, no account to delete‚Äîyou just stop sending. No hard feelings, ever.
